Property Queen Australia (05/2018 – 12/2018)

A platform to show Australia projects for real estate agents and developers. Linking estate owners to purchase or lease with expected
buyers. Propertyqueen.com is Australia’s first map-based web portal to provide home owners,real estate agents and developers with an
advertising platform to display their property.
Link: https://www.propertyqueen.com
